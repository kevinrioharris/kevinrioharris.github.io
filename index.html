About Me

I’m a Data Scientist with a background in Engineering Physics, passionate about transforming data into impactful business solutions. Through Purwadhika’s Data Science & Machine Learning program, industry internships, and self-initiated projects, I've gained hands-on experience in building and deploying end-to-end machine learning pipelines using Python, SQL, Streamlit, and Google Cloud Platform.

I’ve applied these skills to real-world challenges, developing solutions like customer churn prediction models and hotel booking demand forecasting systems—each designed to deliver actionable insights and real business impact. My work emphasizes not just accuracy, but also interpretability and strategic value. I also build interactive dashboards using Tableau and Power BI to support data-driven decisions.

I thrive at the intersection of data, technology, and business, with a strong commitment to delivering solutions that enhance performance, reduce risk, and fuel growth.
